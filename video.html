Round Details

Round 1:
The High-Stakes Quiz

The battle begins with a rapid-fire test of knowledge, logic, and quick thinking. Each team will face a series of challenging questions, covering a wide range of topics from history and science to riddles and lateral thinking. The pressure is on-each question comes with a strict 15-second time limit. Only those who can think on their feet will make it through. At the end of this round, the 10 highest-scoring teams will advance.

Round 2:
The Puzzle Showdown

With only 10 teams remaining, the challenge intensifies. Now, it’s time for a high-pressure, mind-bending 2-minute puzzle that will test not only intelligence but also teamwork and problem-solving skills. Teams must crack codes, decipher cryptic messages, and solve intricate logic puzzles before time runs out. Only the best of the best will make the cut, as just 7 teams will move on to the next stage.

Round 3:
Hunt The Men

Now, the real test begins. The remaining 7 teams will enter a high-stakes 20-minute scavenger hunt, where they must follow a trail of clues, each one more perplexing than the last. The challenges will push teams to their limits, requiring keen observation, deductive reasoning, and lightning-fast decision-making. The competition will be fierce, but in the end, only 3 teams will emerge victorious, proving themselves as the ultimate masterminds of mystery and intelligence.
