The Challenge: X-Mock SuperAgents

X-Mock SuperAgents is structured into three intense rounds, each designed to filter out the sharpest minds and the most effective teams.

Round 1: The High-Stakes Quiz
The journey begins with a rapid-fire knowledge test, where teams must answer a series of challenging questions in just 15 seconds per question. The topics range from general knowledge and logic puzzles to riddles and problem-solving scenarios. This round is designed to test participants’ ability to think quickly and accurately under pressure.

Only the 10 best-performing teams will advance to the next stage, proving their quick-thinking skills and knowledge.

Round 2: The Puzzle Showdown
With only 10 teams remaining, the competition heats up. The second round is a 2-minute puzzle-solving challenge that demands exceptional teamwork, analytical thinking, and problem-solving abilities. Teams must decode cryptic messages, crack codes, and solve intricate puzzles before time runs out.

This round is a true test of collaboration—teams that communicate effectively and divide tasks efficiently have the best chance of success. At the end of this stage, only 7 teams will advance, inching closer to victory.

Round 3: The Ultimate Clue Hunt
The final and most intense stage of the competition is a 20-minute clue hunt, where teams must follow a series of interconnected clues, each one more perplexing than the last. Participants must rely on logic, deduction, and observational skills to unravel the mystery and find the final solution.

This round is designed to simulate real-world problem-solving scenarios, where missing a single detail can mean failure. Only 3 teams will emerge victorious, proving themselves as the ultimate masterminds of the challenge.
